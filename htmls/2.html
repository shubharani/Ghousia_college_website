Admission Details

Admission To B.E. Through Competitive Entrance Test


  • KARNATAKA EXAMINATION AUTHORITY (KEA):


    • (formally called as CET) Karnataka domicile candidates can apply for this test. for details please log on to http://www.kea.kar.nic.in

  • KRLM:

    • Both karnataka and Non-Karnataka candidates can take this test. For details refer to website: www.kmca.info


  • MANAGEMENT:

    • Apply direct to the college during may/june or email: principal_gce@hotmail.com


M.Tech.

Admission is through PGCET only. For details please log on to http://www.kea.kar.nic.in (candidates from Karnataka and Non-Karnataka are eligible)

apply direct to the college or email: principal_gce@hotmail.com
